Top 3 Movies To Watch

According to my Recommendation

The Machinist

The Machinist is a 2004 psychological thriller film directed by Brad Anderson and written by Scott Kosar. It stars Christian Bale as the title character, a machinist struggling with paranoia and delusion after being unable to sleep for an entire year.

Bronson

Michael Peterson, who was initially sentenced to seven years in prison for a robbery, ends up spending thirty years in solitary confinement, where he develops an alter ego, Charles Bronson.

Legends

Identical twins Reggie and Ronnie Kray rise through the ranks of the criminal underworld to become two of London's most dreaded gangsters.
